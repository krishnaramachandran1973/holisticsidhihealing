Linesh Dev Guruvayoor

Linesh Dev was born and brought up near the famous Guruvayoor temple in Thrissur district, Kerala. He was always a pious and devoted person. Few years ago, he realized his thirst for seeking Truth through continuous meditation, prayers and ardent devotion. Many years hence, in the year 2016, he suddenly got the vision of the Devi (Goddess) in his house. Since then, he realized that he had attained extraordinary powers – powers to heal, divine astrology visions and so on. He realized the healing powers of Vibhuti from the sacred fire and Haldi powder duly spiritualized. The Vibhuti and Haldi powder were by products of the worship of the Devi.

He has since been instrumental in healing hitherto incurable illnesses. He also explored other forms of healing – Pranic as well as Tantric. Through this expertise, he can visualize the root cause of problems, and ailments, and diagnose if the problem is at the physical, emotional/mental or spiritual level. . And upon meditating over these problem causes as well as offering the worshipped vibhuti and haldi powder, he was able to resolve all issues of the patient. Through a process of meditation, he could induce positive energy into the bodies of the ailing patients and cause the ailments to be remedied.

Linesh Dev wants to give the benefit of this spiritual gift to the benefit of a wider audience. He already has patients from other districts, states and even foreign nationals.
